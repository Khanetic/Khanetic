## 👤 About Me

Software Engineer at **Myrix Labs**, building agentic AI systems, scalable FastAPI backends, and automated testing pipelines. Pursuing my **Master's in Embedded Systems Engineering** at Fachhochschule Dortmund.

I build things that work at scale — autonomous AI agents, RAG pipelines, LLM-powered tools, and production-grade APIs. I care about clean architecture, fast iteration, and shipping work that lasts. A continuous learner by nature — always exploring the next layer of the stack.
